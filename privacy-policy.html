Privacy

This Privacy Policy shall apply to the applications published in the Apple App Store by Social Hue LLC and that are accessible from mobile devices (the "App").

What Information do we collect?

  • This app collects analytic information related to your social network accounts, such as followers & following amounts. This information is not shared with other users of the app and is visible only by you on your device.
  • This app does not store or have access to information used to login to service providers, such as password and emails. Authentication is done directly by you to the service providers. The app store your session id or OAuth access tokens generated by the service providers to communicate.
  • This app does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.
  • When you contact us to seek our customer support, we may obtain and maintain any information you choose to provide, including your name, your email addresses, as well as information about your mobile device such as its device type OS type and any information concerning your use of the Application.

If you are located in the EU, we may only do so based on legal requirements specified above of EU authorities. This includes sharing such information with our legal counsels.

We encourage you to look for and review the privacy statements of each and every website that you visit and be mindful of the information you may submit to any such third party.
